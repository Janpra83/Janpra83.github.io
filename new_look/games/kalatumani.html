Kalattumani Kaiyattumani

Instructions

  1. 2 teams (A & B), 4-15 members each. Each team selects a captain.
  2. Captains confer and choose different categories (e.g. colours, fruits, vegetables, animals, film stars, sports). Captain names each member with a category name (e.g. Cat, Dog, Parrot). Teams keep names secret; captains have no individual names.
  3. Teams sit opposite each other with legs facing.
  4. Team A's captain kneels behind a child chosen by Team B and covers their eyes.
  5. Others shake legs and hands, chanting "KALATTUMANI KAIYATTUMANI" (Shake legs, shake hands). Captain calls a teammate by their secret name; that person touches the chosen child's head and returns. Captain releases hands and chanting stops.
  6. The chosen child guesses who touched them.
  7. One point for a correct guess; only one guess allowed. Other children must not give clues.
  8. Teams alternate turns.
