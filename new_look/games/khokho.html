Kho Kho

Instructions

  1. Play Kho Kho in 2 teams of 12, in a field approx 30m x 15m.
  2. Start with 8 members of the chasing team sitting or kneeling in a row in the middle, with alternate members facing opposite directions. Two wooden poles/chairs at each end of the central line. The ninth player is the chaser at one pole.
  3. Send 3 members of the other team onto the court. Defenders can be dismissed by: (a) being touched (palm) by a chaser, (b) going out of limits, (c) entering late.
  4. Chasers run in one direction only and cannot cut across the central line; dodgers may run anywhere.
  5. Pass off chasing by touching another sitter whose back faces you while shouting "kho". After catching 3 defenders, send the next 3.
