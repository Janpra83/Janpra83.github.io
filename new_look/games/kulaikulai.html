Kulai Kulaiya Mundrika

Instructions

  1. 10-40 children sit in a circle on the ground.
  2. One child runs around the circle holding a scarf/light material.
  3. This child calls the first line, others respond with the next line.
  4. Call and response continues for the next 4 lines.
  5. On "Vun Vaayila Pongal" the runner throws the scarf onto another child's shoulder. That child chases the runner around the circle to catch them before they reach the vacated spot.
  6. The runner must go around once. If successful, they take the vacant place; otherwise the game continues.
